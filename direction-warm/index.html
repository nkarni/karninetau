A little about me

I've spent my career in the space between people, business and technology — as a founder, product director and de-facto CTO. What I enjoy most isn't the code; it's reframing a contradictory, "either/or" problem into something a team can actually move on.

That instinct comes from an unusual path: I trained as an oboist and instrument repairer before IT, I'm an accredited mediator and an Alexander Technique teacher, and I've led volunteer committees — most recently as president of a paragliding club — through real change. It all points the same way: getting a group of people to a good outcome together.
